About Me

My Journey

I come from India and have always loved mathematics, which eventually led me to pursue engineering at IIT Kharagpur, where I earned a Bachelor's and Master's in Electrical Engineering.

During university, I explored machine learning and computer vision through projects and research internships. In 2017, I was among two students selected for internships at the University of Tokyo and DMG Mori, where I worked on deep learning and image processing. These experiences not only shaped my passion for AI but also sparked my long-standing connection with Japan.

After joining Mercari as a new graduate, I grew from an ML engineer to an engineering manager, leading teams across machine learning, search, and platform reliability domains.
